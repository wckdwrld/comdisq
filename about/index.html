The Project

COMDISQ is a localised community discussion platform built upon a Raspberry-Pi supported SMS server. The goal of the project is to produce a software and hardware product that considers the complexities of interaction in social media and the inevitable alienation of users while partaking in such interactions. In turn, the final product will provide a space wherein the prosumer is no longer exploited for their social content and is encouraged to interact for the betterment of the community as oppose to material gain of corporation.

The Dream is to have built a tool that allows others to provide a foundation for local community interaction whereby users can discuss difficult topics with their counterparts.
